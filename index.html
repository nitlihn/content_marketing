The healthy choice

Like coconut water, bamboo is naturally hydrating. It’s sugar-free and contains only five calories per 100ml. Bamboo boosts natural collagen production, helping to inhibit the ageing process and is one of the strongest natural sources of silica – the mineral which helps support healthy skin, hair and nails. Bonus: bamboo is one of the fastest-growing plants on the planet, making this drink more environmentally-friendly than other tree waters.
